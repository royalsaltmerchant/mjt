GREETINGS FELLOW EARTHLINGS!

Whether your musical tastes run towards world beat, jazz, rock, soul, funk or even baroque, open your auditory passages and explore the cosmic creations of this eclectic East Bay trio!

You will find one of a kind compositions spontaneously created and curated for the open-minded listener.

Sit back, fasten your metaphysical seat belts and prepare to be transported to the farthest reaches of your mind!
